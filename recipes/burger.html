Burger

Description

Create the most iconic sandwich quickly, just cook the patty and start assembling. Bread and meat are the minimum requirements and the rest is optional.

Ingredients

  • Brioche bun
  • Burger patty
  • Pickled cucumber
  • Sriracha garlic mayo
  • Cheese slice(s) of choice
  • Caramelized onions

Directions

  1. Cook the patty on a hot pan.
  2. Flip patty after 2 minutes.
  3. Add cheese on top of the patty.
  4. Toast brioche buns from the inside using rendered fat.
  5. Apply sriracha garlic mayo to toasted buns.
  6. Add pickles and onions.
  7. Add the burger patty.
